DAVID BRYNE is a Scottish-born American singer, songwriter, musician, record producer, artist, actor, writer, music theorist, and filmmaker who is a founding member and the principal songwriter, lead singer and guitarist of Talking Heads.

CHRIS FRANTZ is an American musician and record producer. He is the drummer for both Talking Heads and Tom Tom Club, which he co-founded with wife and Talking Heads bassist Tina Weymouth.

JERRY HARRISON is the keyboardist and guitarist for Talking Heads and is an original member of the Modern Lovers.

TINA WEYMOUTH is a founding member and bassist of the Talking Heads and it's side project Tom Tom Club.
